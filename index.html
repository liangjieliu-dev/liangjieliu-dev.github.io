About Me

I am an MSc student in Data-Driven Modeling and a part-time Research Assistant in the Department of Industrial Engineering and Decision Analytics at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). Concurrently, I am interning at Tencent's Weixin Group, where I focus on data analysis and optimization tasks for recommendation systems.

My primary research interests lie in understanding and optimizing platform operations. I investigate the causal evaluation and optimization of platform mechanisms, particularly in recommendation systems. Methodologically, I am passionate about machine learning, causal inference, and social network analysis, aiming to develop innovative solutions for real-world problems.

Previously, I earned my B.S. in Mathematics from Wuhan University in 2023, where my undergraduate thesis on graph neural networks for spatial transcriptomics was advised by Prof. Suoqin Jin. During 2023–2024, I worked full-time as a software engineer at the Shenzhen Institute of Computing Sciences, developing database tools and APIs to support advanced computational applications.
